*Connections*
ESP32 PIN CONNECTIONS
=====================

ADXL345 (Accelerometer):
  VCC    → 3.3V
  GND    → GND
  SDA    → GPIO 21
  SCL    → GPIO 22

SSD1306 OLED Display:
  VCC    → 3.3V
  GND    → GND
  SDA    → GPIO 21 (same as ADXL345)
  SCL    → GPIO 22 (same as ADXL345)

Buzzer:
  (+)    → GPIO 2
  (-)    → GND

LED:
  (+)    → GPIO 4 (through 220Ω resistor)
  (-)    → GND

Power:
  USB    → ESP32 Micro USB port
